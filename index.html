Infosys Full Stack Sales Leadership Program


Today buyers are performing extensive research and many are deciding on solutions before engaging a salesperson or having a conversation. Forrester forecasts that “1 million B2B salespeople will lose their jobs to self-service eCommerce by the year 2020”.
Being in an experience economy, customer experience is a critical priority, second only to growing revenue and that is seen to be lacking. Buyers today are bombarded with opinions and options. There can be a multitude of ways to solve an issue and navigating the best path cab be challenging. Also, matrixed, global organizations, the desire for collaboration and buy-in, and increased risk aversion have driven buyers to bring more stakeholders into the buying process, adding to the complexity.

Operating in a volatile, uncertain, complex and ambiguous (VUCA) environment makes it hard to determine what long-term strategies and short-term tactics will be most effective in reaching business goals and whether those goals are still relevant. Customer satisfaction is no longer equivalent to customer loyalty. A customer may say they are satisfied and still switch to a competitor. Ensuring the customer stays with you is critical. Buyers still seek trust, authenticity and clarity, from their interactions with salespersons, to help them synthesize value from their resources.
McKinsey research found that B2B companies averaged less than 50% on a customer-experience index rating. Sellers facing the pressures of this challenging world, may often dig in their heels and resort to seller-centric behaviors to try to strong-arm customers or gloss over core issues. To respond to these dynamic areas and issues, a Full Stack Sales Leader is needed.

A full stack sales leader is the one who has a blend of sales and behavioral skills, knows their industry & markets and is familiar with the niche technologies in their domains. A specific set of courses have been identified in consultation with Sales Leaders in each of these areas. To become a Full Stack Sales Leader, you will need to complete all the courses in Sales, Behavioral and Technical skills and select the areas under Industry/Markets that are relevant to you.
